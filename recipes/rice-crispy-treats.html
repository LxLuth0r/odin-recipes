Rice Crispy Treats

Description

This recipe is for Grandma's homemade rice crispy treat's. Made up of only 3 ingredients and take approximately 10-15 minutes to make.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 16 oz marshmallows
  • 6 cups rice crispies

Steps

  1. Melt butter in large pot over med-low heat
  2. Add marshmallows and stir continually until completely melted and golden
  3. Remove from heat and stir in 6 cups of rice crispies
  4. Spread evenly onto parchment paper and enjoy!
