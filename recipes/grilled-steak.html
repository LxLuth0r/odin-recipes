Grilled Steak

Description

This recipe is for a perfectly grilled steak in under 10 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 1 Ribeye/NY Strip Steak
  • Salt & seasoning of your choice

Steps

  1. Season steak all over with salt and any seasonings of your choice. I prefer salt, pepper, and some ground coffee.
  2. Heat grill until it reaches at least 450 degress, but the hotter the better.
  3. Grill steak on each side for 3-4 minutes.
  4. Check steak with meat thermometer and once it reaches an internal temperature of 130 degrees for medium-rare, remove from grill.
  5. Let steak rest for 6-8 minutes.
  6. Enjoy!
