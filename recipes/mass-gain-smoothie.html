Mass Gain Smoothie

Description

This recipe is for Ronnie Coleman's mass gain smoothie.

Ingredients

  • 2 heaping scoops of mass gain whey protein
  • 3 tablespoons of peanut butter
  • 1 banana
  • 24 oz of water
  • 6-12 ice cubes

Steps

  1. Add ice cubes, water, banana, peanut butter, and whey protein to a blender
  2. Blend on high until smooth
  3. Lightweight, baby!
